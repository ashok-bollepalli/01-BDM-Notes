# Data Analytics Leads Handling Points

## Course Overview

- Data Analytics is a job-oriented course for students, freshers, job seekers, and working professionals.
- This course helps students learn how to collect, clean, analyze, visualize, and present data.
- Students will learn Excel, SQL, Python, Power BI, statistics basics, dashboards, reports, and real-time projects.
- Goal: prepare students for Data Analyst, Business Analyst, MIS Analyst, Reporting Analyst, and Power BI Developer roles.

## Who Can Join

- B.Tech, MCA, BCA, B.Sc, B.Com, MBA, Degree students
- Freshers and job seekers
- Non-IT students interested in analytics jobs
- Working professionals planning to switch into Data Analytics
- Sales, finance, operations, HR, support, and marketing professionals who work with data
- Anyone interested in reports, dashboards, and business insights

## Pre-Requisites

- Basic computer knowledge is required.
- Coding knowledge is not mandatory.
- Basic Excel knowledge is helpful but not mandatory.
- Laptop/Desktop and internet connection are required.
- Logical thinking and interest in data are important.
- Daily practice is required.

## Common Lead Questions

### Can I join without coding knowledge?

Yes, coding knowledge is not mandatory. Python will be covered from basics.

### Is this course suitable for non-IT students?

Yes, Data Analytics is suitable for both IT and non-IT students.

### Do I need strong mathematics?

No, only basic statistics and logical thinking are required.

### Do you cover Power BI?

Yes, Power BI dashboards, reports, Power Query, and DAX basics are covered.

### Do you provide projects?

Yes, real-time Data Analytics projects and dashboard projects are included.

### Do you provide recordings?

Yes, backup videos are provided for revision.

### Do you provide placement?

Yes, placement assistance, resume guidance, job updates, and interview preparation are provided.

### What kind of projects will students build?

Students will work on sales analysis, customer analysis, HR analytics, finance reports, business dashboards, and real-time reporting projects.

### How much practice is required?

Minimum 2 to 3 hours daily practice is recommended.
